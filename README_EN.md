**UcardPay** is a cryptocurrency card issuance and payment management system. It is designed to help you quickly build your own crypto card distribution platform, supporting bulk issuance of Visa/Mastercard virtual and physical cards, automatic USDT deposits, real-time fiat settlement, and a complete agent distribution system.
Similar to crypto card projects like THPAY / WildCard.

**UcardPay** is a digital wallet and card issuance service platform (Virtual/Physical Crypto Card Platform, Crypto Wallet, similar to Alipay).

1. **Core Service**: Primarily provides Visa / MasterCard virtual and physical card services to users.

2. **Crypto Card Model**: Supports cryptocurrency deposits (e.g., USDT), which are automatically converted into fiat currency (USD/HKD, etc.) and deposited into the card.

3. **Global Payments**: Issued cards can be used globally at online merchants supporting Visa/Mastercard (Amazon, Netflix, OpenAI, etc.) and offline POS terminals.

4. **Major Payment Integration**: Supports binding with **Alipay** and **WeChat Pay** for consumption, addressing a core need for many users.

5. **Multi-Currency Account**: Supports exchange and management of multiple currencies such as USD, HKD, and CNY.

6. **Financial Services**: Supports wealth management modes, flash exchange, payments/collections, internal transfers, currency exchange, and crypto purchasing.

7. **Mobile Wallets**: Supports **Apple Pay** and **Google Pay**.

8. **Dual Clients**: Available for both Android and iOS.
